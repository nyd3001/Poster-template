\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Motivation}: Existing audio captioning datasets are limited in scale, lexical diversity, and domain coverage, hindering the development of generalizable audio understanding models.
    \item \textbf{Solution}: ACAVCaps is introduced --- a large-scale, fine-grained audio captioning dataset derived from ACAV100M.
    \item \textbf{Advantages}:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{Data Source}: A novel data source (ACAV100M), entirely distinct from existing captioning datasets (e.g., AudioSet).
        \item \textbf{Pipeline}: Multi-expert analysis + LLM Chain-of-Thought reasoning for rich, multi-faceted captions.
        \item \textbf{Audio Coverage}: Diverse audio domains, including speech, music, sound, and their various combinations.
        \item \textbf{Text Coverage}: Multi-perspective captions spanning systematic, content-related, and content-unrelated dimensions.
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
